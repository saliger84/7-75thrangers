How to Enlist

  1. Read the About page and confirm the unit fits your schedule and expectations.
  2. Join the community Discord or TeamSpeak and introduce yourself.
  3. Submit your application and complete any required interview.
  4. Attend orientation, initial training, and your first scheduled drill.

Requirements

Maturity

Professional conduct and teamwork matter more than raw playtime.

Availability

You should be able to attend required events and communicate when absent.

Willingness to Learn

New members are expected to complete training and follow SOPs.
