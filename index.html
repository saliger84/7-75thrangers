RICH HISTORY

The 7-75th Ranger Regiment Realism Unit is built on discipline, structure, and consistent operations with a focus on immersive, authentic gameplay.

Members are expected to maintain standards, develop leadership, and contribute to a professional milsim environment.

WEEKLY EVENTS

Operations, squad drills, and training nights are scheduled consistently so members always know what is next and can stay mission ready.

Weekly activity keeps tactics sharp, reinforces standards, and strengthens cohesion across the unit.

SPECIALIZATIONS

Members can advance through infantry, leadership, support, reconnaissance, medic, aviation, and administrative tracks as they progress.

Each role supports the overall mission and gives members a clear path for development and responsibility.
