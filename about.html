About Us

Who We Are

The 7-75th Ranger Regiment is a realism-focused milsim community that values professionalism, teamwork, and consistent weekly participation. Our goal is to create an immersive military experience without losing sight of community and long-term member growth.

Standards

Members are expected to communicate clearly, follow chain of command, and maintain discipline during events.

Operations

We run scheduled operations, drills, and administrative events to keep the unit structured and active.

Community

We emphasize mentorship, leadership development, and a strong culture of mutual respect.

What We Expect

  • Reliable attendance for operations and mandatory events.
  • Maturity, discipline, and respect for fellow members.
  • Willingness to train, improve, and support the team.
  • Commitment to immersion and unit standards.
